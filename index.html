About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at King Abdullah University of Science and Technology (KAUST), where I am a member of the High-Performance Visualization Group advised by Prof. Markus Hadwiger. I received my B.E. in Computer Science from the Honor College of University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) in 2020.

My primary research interests lie in Scientific Visualization and Computer Graphics. I am also passionate about related fields, including AI-driven Visual Computing, High Dynamic Range (HDR) Imaging, Deep Learning for LiDAR data, Point Cloud Learning, and Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs).
