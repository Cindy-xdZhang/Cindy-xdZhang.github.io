Publications

  • This work studies the efficient Synthesis scheme of HDR pictures, we propose a method to quickly produce synthetic weights according to the intermediate products of JPEG image compression. Publication of research paper (Multi-exposure Fusion with JPEG Compression Guidance) as first author was accepted by IEEE VCIP2018 (Visual Communications and Images Processing).

  • We focus on a deep learning solution for deep detection complement in on-board scenarios, we use virtual engine (UE4) to generate synthetic training data, train our model with our own multichannel multi-scale fusion depth module. Research paper （DeepLiDAR: Deep Surface Normal Guided Depth Prediction for Outdoor Scene from Sparse LiDAR Data and Single Color Image） was accepted by CVPR2019 (Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition).
